Our Story

Basketball Zone was founded in 2024 in Quezon City, Metro Manila, with one simple goal: to make basketball accessible, professional, and inspiring for everyone. Starting with small training sessions, we have grown into a trusted sports organization focused on skill development, teamwork, and community building.

We believe that basketball teaches discipline, leadership, and resilience — values that help players succeed both on and off the court. Our team consists of certified coaches, sports managers, and passionate supporters working together to grow the game in the Philippines.

Future Business & Vision

Where we are expanding — building a sustainable, growing basketball brand

📈 Business Expansion Plan

  • Multi-Center Facilities: Build full-size indoor courts, gyms, and training hubs across major cities in the Philippines.
  • Academy & Development Program: Create a structured youth academy to discover and develop future professional players.
  • Digital Platform: Launch an online portal for bookings, live scores, coaching content, and e-commerce store.
  • Partnerships & Sponsorships: Collaborate with schools, corporations, and sports brands to expand reach and funding.
  • International Exposure: Organize youth exchanges, friendly matches, and exposure trips to global leagues.

🚀 Coming Soon Services

  • Sports Science & Fitness: Physical therapy, nutrition planning, and performance analysis for athletes.
  • Broadcasting & Media: Live streaming of tournaments, highlights, and sports news content.
  • Event Management: Hosting large-scale basketball events, charity games, and celebrity matches.
  • Community Outreach: Free clinics for underprivileged youth and school sports development support.
  • Franchise Opportunities: Allow qualified partners to open Basketball Zone branches in their regions.

📅 3-Year Growth Roadmap

Year 1

Establish core operations, build customer base, and launch digital presence.

Year 2

Open first full facility, expand leagues, and introduce merchandise line.

Year 3+

Expand to multiple locations, launch academy programs, and start franchise model.
